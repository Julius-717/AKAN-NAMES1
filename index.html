Ghanaian Names

About

This is a web application to determine your name with the week that you were born.

Akan name is a Ghanaian form of naming.You are named according to the week that you were born and gender.Like for example Kwame Nkurumah was named Kwame because he was born on a saturday which is Kwame.

In here you will be able to figure out your name with the day you were born.
